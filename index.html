Welcome!

I'm Stefanie Engstrom, an IT professional-turned-aspiring software engineer.

My time working in IT has made me deeply passionate about:

  • creating efficient tools that can make everyone's lives easier;
  • fostering trust by building useful products with clear, accessible documentation;
  • communicating effectively to understand the needs of stakeholders before creating a realistic plan to address those needs.

About Me

I've spent years working in a variety of professions -- from academic proofreading, to working professionally as a referee, to even dabbling in wine sales before, ultimately, finding my way to IT and systems engineering.

If there's one thing that every role I've ever taken on has required, it's a certain human touch -- someone to identify and bridge gaps in knowledge, planning, and execution as they arise, and help make anyone from a client, to an executive, to an Olympic athlete feel that they're valued and understood.

At the same time, as a lifelong tech enthusiast and longterm tech professional, I know firsthand the frustration and confusion that unclear errors, broken features, or a lack of accessibility can cause on a website or in an app.

As an engineer, I want to be a part of the solution -- and help use technology to help build better tools and features for us all.
